Dignans is a high quality outside catering company based in Edinburgh. They have been established for five years and work from a café/brassiere which is the other side of their business. Currently they have regular customers and several contracts for catering. These are from local businesses and offices in the immediate area surrounding the café, from where the catering is based. In addition they get one off clients requiring services like celebration buffets. Often these customers have come from word of mouth recommendations from previous customers. The company advertise themselves only by adverts on their company vehicle, business cards, and through local directories. The company are at the stage that they would like to expand the business further afield, but within Edinburgh district into office lunches, corporate catering, corporate barbeques, etc and would like to target a wider customer base in and around Edinburgh.
